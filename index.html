What We Work On

Quantari supports the preparation and structuring of financing opportunities so that engagement with banks, DFIs, private credit and institutional investors can proceed with coherence and execution clarity.

The focus is not on capital access in isolation, but on ensuring transactions are prepared in a way capital can respond to.

Working Capital & Trade Financing

Structuring facilities aligned to operating cycles and institutional credit expectations.

Expansion & Capex Financing

Preparing financing propositions that withstand lender and investor scrutiny.

Debt Structuring & Restructuring

Clarifying positions and pathways where financing complexity exists.

Credit & Investment Readiness

Strengthening preparedness before engagement with institutional capital providers.

How We Approach Transactions

Transactions are shaped long before they reach a credit committee or investment desk. Clarity of cashflows, risk allocation, financing structure and engagement discipline often determines whether capital is deployed, delayed or withdrawn.

Our work focuses on that preparatory layer — where confidence is built and outcomes are influenced.

Our Role in the Capital Ecosystem

Quantari does not lend, invest or intermediate capital.

We operate upstream — supporting how transactions are structured, articulated and prepared before they reach capital providers across SMEs, lenders, DFIs and private credit environments.

Leadership

Quantari Advisory Partners was founded by Oliver Gitahi after more than 17 years in corporate and SME banking.

Across that time, a consistent pattern emerged, the difference between financeable and non-financeable situations often lay not in the business itself, but in how transactions were structured and engaged.

Quantari was established to work directly within that space.
