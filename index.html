Who We Work With

We work with operating SMEs and mid-market businesses preparing for structured engagement with institutional financing — as well as lenders, partners and stakeholders navigating complex financing situations.

What We Do

Transaction Structuring

Supporting the preparation and structuring of opportunities so they are aligned to institutional credit and investment expectations before capital engagement begins.

Credit & Investment Readiness

Preparing businesses for engagement with banks, DFIs and private capital through disciplined articulation of cashflows, risks and financing requirements.

Lender & Investor Engagement

Supporting structured dialogue, information flows and due diligence preparation during financing processes.

Debt Positioning & Restructuring

Clarifying financing arrangements and structuring pathways where complexity or misalignment exists.

Our Role in the Capital Ecosystem

Quantari operates upstream of financing — supporting how transactions are structured, articulated and prepared before they reach lenders, DFIs and institutional capital providers. The focus is on preparation discipline, transaction clarity and engagement coherence.

Leadership

Quantari Advisory Partners was founded by Oliver Gitahi after more than 17 years in corporate and SME banking, structuring and managing financing relationships across operating businesses and institutional capital providers.

His work focuses on bringing disciplined structuring, institutional perspective and transaction clarity into financing preparation.
