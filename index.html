What We Do

Quantari is a corporate finance and transaction advisory platform focused on improving the quality and preparedness of financing opportunities before capital engagement begins.

Many viable businesses struggle to access institutional capital not because they are unbankable, but because transactions are not structured to lender and investor expectations.

Transaction Structuring

Aligning cashflows, risk allocation and financing structures with institutional credit and investment expectations.

Credit & Investment Readiness

Preparing businesses for engagement with banks, DFIs and private capital providers.

Lender & Investor Engagement

Supporting management teams through due diligence and structured financing discussions.

Debt Optimisation & Restructuring

Improving sustainability, clarity and alignment of existing financing arrangements.

Why Quantari

Most advisory support begins after capital has already been approached. By then, structural weaknesses are difficult to correct.

Quantari operates earlier in the process — shaping transactions before lender engagement, aligning business narratives with institutional credit thinking, and preparing management teams for disciplined engagement.

Our Role in the Capital Ecosystem

Quantari does not lend, invest, broker or distribute capital.

Our role is to improve how transactions are prepared, articulated and structured before they reach capital providers — across SMEs, commercial banks, DFIs, private credit and structured finance environments.

Leadership

Quantari Advisory Partners was founded by Oliver Gitahi after more than 17 years in corporate and SME banking, structuring and managing financing relationships across operating businesses and institutional capital providers.

His work focuses on bringing disciplined structuring, institutional perspective and transaction clarity into financing preparation.
