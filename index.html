Where We Operate

Our work sits between operating businesses and institutional capital — supporting the preparation and structuring of transactions so engagement with banks, development finance institutions, private credit and structured capital providers is grounded and execution-ready.

The objective is not access to capital in itself, but ensuring opportunities are articulated and structured in a way capital can respond to.

What This Looks Like in Practice

Working Capital & Trade Finance Structuring

Aligning facility structures with operating cycles and lender expectations.

Expansion & Capex Financing Preparation

Structuring propositions capable of withstanding institutional credit scrutiny.

Debt Positioning & Restructuring

Clarifying financing arrangements where complexity exists.

Credit & Investment Readiness

Preparing opportunities for engagement with banks, DFIs and private capital.

Lender & Investor Engagement Support

Supporting structured dialogue and due diligence preparation.

Approach

Transactions are shaped long before they reach a credit committee or investment desk.

Clarity of cashflows, risk allocation, financing structure and engagement discipline often determines whether capital is deployed, delayed or withdrawn.

Our work focuses on that preparatory layer — where confidence is built and financing processes begin to move with coherence.
